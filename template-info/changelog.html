Mobile Menu

To change the mobile menu, first go to the tablet breakpoint at the top of your screen, make sure you choose the tablet breakpoint so that your changes have an impact on smaller screens.

Edit Projects

In the CMS collection, you have the Projects side. It can have another name but most of the time it is called Projects. Upload the projects you want in the collection, everything is explained so that you can manage your template in an efficient way.

Images Blocks

Sometimes images are handled in a particular way. They are not handled by the elements panel to give it background properties. If this happens, here's what to do. Go to the style panel on the right of your screen.

Look for the Backgrounds section. If it has an image, click on it.

Then change it with the image of your choice.
