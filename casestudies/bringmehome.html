Bring Me Home

Bring Me Home (BMH) is an app designed to reduce food wastage and support local businesses. 1 of every 5 meals is thrown out yet 1 in every 6 Australians experiences food insecurity. Bring Me Home makes food more accessible to the people who need it. On average, cafes and restaurants bin over 100 kgs of food every week, over half of which could have been prevented. Food waste from Australian businesses produces the equivalent of almost half a million tonnes of CO2 per year.
I am proud of my work on this project because not only was it for a cause I am passionate about, but also it was my final end-to-end design project for my UX bootcamp. From research and ideation, to prototyping and eventually coding the final product.
