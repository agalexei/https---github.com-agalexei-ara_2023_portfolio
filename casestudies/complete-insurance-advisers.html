Complete Insurance Advisers NSW

In my most recent freelance endeavor, I played a pivotal role in the creation and development of a website for an up-and-coming insurance brokerage startup. Our primary objective was to craft a digital platform that not only enhances user experience but also empowers individuals and businesses in their pursuit of trusted insurance brokers and comprehensive coverage. Our dedication to user-centric design and seamless functionality has resulted in a website that simplifies the process of finding the right insurance solutions for any business.
Process
As a key contributor to the successful design and development of a website for an emerging insurance brokerage startup, I undertook a strategic approach to ensure the project's success. My initial steps included a comprehensive competitor analysis, leveraging SWOT analysis, to identify gaps and opportunities in the market. Concurrently, I conducted in-depth user interviews to gain valuable insights into the pain points users encountered when dealing with competitors. This dual-pronged approach not only allowed us to pinpoint areas for improvement but also served as the foundation for our user-centric design strategy.

Furthermore, I was actively involved in the prototyping and website development phases, utilizing HTML and CSS with the Bootstrap framework to create an intuitive and visually appealing user interface. This hands-on experience enabled me to translate our design concepts into a functional and responsive website, ensuring that our users' needs were met seamlessly in the final product.
The Problem
Business owners looking at boutique insurance brokage websites are struggling to find relevant information, leading to high bounce rates or users looking elsewhere. Many sites laack intuitive navigation and many users often abandon out of frustration due to the complexity or thinking the brokerages do not meet their needs.
The Solution
We propose addressing these needs and enhancing the user experience of Complete Insurance Advisers' website by laying out a comprehensive design, focused on improving navigation, content clarity and also making it visually aesthetic.
Research
We initially defined our objectives which were;
  • Acquiring new customers
  • Providing insurance
  • Becoming a serious service provider in the brokerage industry
  • Business growth

Secondly, we conducted user interviews to help identify udser pain points, user needs, as well as work out who our users were.
Pain points:
  • No call back from broker after enquiry
  • Unhappy claims experience
  • Feeling that needs are not being met

User needs:
  • Get insurance
  • Assurance in having the right cover
  • Dedicated help with claims process

We also identified the ideal users were general insurance customers, SMEs and corporate business clients.

Next Steps
As we approach the final phase of this project, our next steps are clear. We will initiate the website's launch, carefully monitoring its performance and user engagement. To gather valuable insights, we will implement robust analytics tools, enabling us to collect and analyze metrics to assess the site's effectiveness.

Moreover, we are committed to an ongoing process of refinement. User testing will be a fundamental aspect of our strategy, ensuring that the website consistently meets user needs and expectations. Simultaneously, we will remain vigilant in identifying and addressing any areas for improvement. This iterative approach underscores our dedication to delivering a website that not only launches successfully but evolves to excel in the dynamic landscape of insurance brokering.

In conclusion, this project is a testament to our commitment to user-centric design and continuous improvement. By launching the site and proactively refining it through data-driven insights, we are poised to make a meaningful impact in the insurance brokerage industry, providing a seamless experience for our users.
