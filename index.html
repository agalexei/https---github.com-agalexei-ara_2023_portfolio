My

Process

01 — Discover

I start by digging deep into the problem. This includes stakeholder conversations, user research, journey mapping, and identifying pain points and opportunities that matter.

02 — Design

With insights in hand, I ideate, sketch, wireframe, and prototype. I focus on clarity, usability, and aligning with brand and business goals—balancing creativity with structure.

03 — Refine

Through testing, feedback, and iteration, I fine-tune the experience. I collaborate closely with developers to ensure smooth handover and polish the final product for real-world use.
