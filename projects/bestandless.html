Best&Less

Best & Less is an Australian national retailer, operating in almost 200 store locations and online, with one of the biggest offerings of baby products nationally.

I was the sole UX/UI designer on this project. It was one of my first commercial projects where I took full ownership of the design process. I worked closely with the optimisation and e-commerce teams, as well as front-end developers to ensure the final product fulfilled the clients' needs.
